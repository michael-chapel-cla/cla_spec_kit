# Customer Personas — LiquidityEventOS

## 1. Founder
Has concentrated private-company wealth, limited time, and high downside if planning is late.

## 2. Pre-IPO Employee
Has options or RSUs, unclear exercise/tax strategy, and anxiety around timing.

## 3. Startup CPA / Advisor
Needs repeatable workflows to serve equity-rich clients at scale.

## 4. Founder-Focused Multi-Disciplinary Firm
Coordinates legal, tax, and wealth advice and needs a single source of truth.
