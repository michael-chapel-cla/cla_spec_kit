- Market size: 6/10
- Ease of build: 5/10

## Summary
The pain is acute and high-value, but the market is narrower and trust requirements are intense. This could become a premium specialist business, but it is probably not the easiest first company to build.

## Why it can win
- extremely high stakes
- clients will pay for expensive mistakes avoided
- strong referral and niche-authority dynamics

## Why it’s harder
- episodic demand
- legal/tax complexity
- higher expectation of expert-grade precision from day one
