# Pitch — LiquidityEventOS

Liquidity events don’t forgive late planning.

Founders and early employees face life-changing choices around options, QSBS, tenders, trusts, AMT, and concentration risk. Today, those decisions are handled through bespoke spreadsheets, fragmented advisors, and panic.

LiquidityEventOS is the planning operating system for equity-rich people before money turns liquid. It organizes grants, models scenarios, surfaces planning opportunities, and coordinates advisor action before the window closes.

The wedge is startup-focused CPAs and advisors. The moat is equity-specific rules logic plus action workflow at the exact moment stakes are highest.
