# Competitive Analysis — LiquidityEventOS

## Category map
- Equity management: Carta, Pulley
- Broad planning software: eMoney, RightCapital
- Niche founder tax advisors and content firms
- spreadsheets and bespoke internal models

## Vendor-by-vendor comparison
### Carta / Pulley
- Strength: equity recordkeeping, cap tables, grants
- Weakness: limited full-spectrum tax and wealth scenario orchestration
- Gap to exploit: turn records into planning actions

### Broad planning tools
- Strength: general wealth context
- Weakness: weak grant-level and liquidity-workflow specificity
- Gap to exploit: event-specific planning logic

### Boutique expert workflows
- Strength: depth and trust
- Weakness: expensive, bespoke, unscalable
- Gap to exploit: productize expert process without losing rigor

## Opportunity summary
LiquidityEventOS should own the pre-liquidity planning workflow that currently lives in fragile spreadsheets and expert heads.
